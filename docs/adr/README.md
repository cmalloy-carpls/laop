# Architecture Decision Records

Significant architectural decisions, captured so a future contributor understands *why* we chose what we chose — not just what we chose.

| # | Title | Status |
|---|---|---|
| [0001](./0001-stack-selection.md) | Stack selection | Accepted |

## Format

- **Context** — what forced the decision
- **Decision** — what we chose
- **Consequences** — trade-offs accepted
- **Alternatives considered** — what we didn't pick and why
- **Status** — Proposed / Accepted / Superseded by #N

Keep them scannable. An ADR should be readable in under 2 minutes.
